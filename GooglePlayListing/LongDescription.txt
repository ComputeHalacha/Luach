Luach is a full featured Jewish Calendar application.

It has special features for calculating a Jewish woman's dates, Kavuahs and times that need to be kept.
It automatically calculates these dates and Kavuahs.

Halachic specifications can be customized.

Luach also includes a full set of Zmanim for anywhere in the world.

You can view the Luach instructions at https://www.compute.co.il/luach/app/
