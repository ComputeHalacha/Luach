Luach keeps track and automatically calculates a Jewish woman's dates, and times that need to be observed.

It automatically calculates and keeps track of patterns (Kavuahs).
All the Halachic specifications can be fully customized.

Luach also functions as a Zmanim and Event Calendar, and it includes a full set of daily Zmanim for anywhere in the world.
It includes event management functionality to keep track of birthdays, Yahrtzeits, special dates and appointments etc.

For a comprehensive explanation about how to use Luach, see the Luach documentation at <a href="https://www.compute.co.il/luach/app/">compute.co.il/luach/app/</a>.
